## Creating Your Account

Getting started with Jasin is simple and free. You'll get 5 lifetime embed credits to try out the service.

### Sign Up Process

1. Visit [getjasin.com](https://getjasin.com)
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email address and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Start creating embeds immediately!

<Info>
  Your free account includes 5 lifetime embed credits. Once you use them, you can upgrade to Pro for 25 credits per month.
</Info>

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is where you'll manage all your product embeds and view analytics.

### Main Features

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Create Embed** | Paste Amazon URLs and generate new product cards |
| **My Embeds** | View and manage all your existing embeds |
| **Analytics** | Track views, clicks, and performance |
| **Settings** | Manage your account and subscription |

## Account Settings

### Profile Information
- **Email Address**: Update your email for account notifications
- **Password**: Change your password for security
- **Display Name**: Optional display name for your account

### Subscription Management
- **Current Plan**: View your current plan (Free or Pro)
- **Usage**: See how many credits you've used
- **Billing**: Manage your Pro subscription (if applicable)

## Upgrading to Pro

### Pro Plan Benefits
- **25 credits per month** instead of 5 lifetime
- **Custom affiliate tags** to earn commissions
- **Advanced analytics** with bot filtering
- **Priority support** for faster help

### How to Upgrade
1. Go to your dashboard settings
2. Click "Upgrade to Pro"
3. Choose monthly or annual billing
4. Complete the secure checkout process
5. Start using Pro features immediately

## Account Limits

### Free Plan Limits
- **5 total embeds** for the lifetime of your account
- **Basic themes** only (light, dark, auto)
- **Standard analytics** (views and clicks)

### Pro Plan Limits
- **25 new embeds per month** (existing embeds continue working)
- **All themes** and customization options
- **Advanced analytics** with detailed insights

<Warning>
  If you downgrade from Pro to Free, your existing embeds will continue working, but you won't be able to create new ones until the next month or until you upgrade again.
</Warning>

## Getting Help

### Support Options
- **Help Center**: Browse common questions and guides
- **Email Support**: Contact support@getjasin.com for assistance
- **Pro Support**: Pro users get priority email support

### Common Issues
- **Forgot Password**: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page
- **Email Not Verified**: Check your spam folder and resend verification
- **Billing Questions**: Contact support with your account email

## Data & Privacy

### Your Data
- **Embeds**: All your created embeds and their analytics
- **Account Info**: Email, password (encrypted), and subscription details
- **Usage Data**: How you use the service to improve our product

### Data Export
You can request an export of your data at any time by contacting support. This includes:
- All your embed data
- Analytics history
- Account information

### Account Deletion
To delete your account:
1. Contact support@getjasin.com
2. Confirm your identity
3. We'll permanently delete your account and data within 30 days

<Info>
  Deleting your account will stop all your embeds from working. Make sure to remove them from your websites first.
</Info>
