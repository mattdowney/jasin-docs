## What is Jasin?

Jasin is a simple tool that transforms Amazon product URLs into beautiful, embeddable product cards for your website or blog. Just paste an Amazon link, customize the appearance, and get a simple `<script>` tag to embed anywhere.

## How It Works

### 1. Create Your Embed
Visit [getjasin.com](https://getjasin.com) and paste any Amazon product URL. You'll see a live preview of your product card.

### 2. Customize & Copy
Choose your theme, customize the appearance, then copy the generated `<script>` tag.

### 3. Embed Anywhere
Paste the script tag into your website, blog, or anywhere HTML is supported. The product card will load automatically.

### 4. Track Performance
View analytics in your dashboard to see how your product cards are performing.
